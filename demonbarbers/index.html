Why choose us?

Low Cost Haircuts

At The Demon Barber, we understand that not everyone can afford to spend excessive amounts of money on high quality haircuts. We strive to be one of the most economical choices in Barry, while also providing a service equivalent to that of a professional salon. Our haircuts start from as cheap as 5 pound depending on the cut required.

Long Term Professionals

All of the members of The Demon Barber have been at the forefront of hairdressing for over a decade. We have seen trends come and go and therefore can produce any hairstyle you like. Whether you're looking for a more modern look, or want something more classic, we will always be able to provide a professional insight.

We Cater To All Ages

These days, many hairdressers tend to cater to a certain demographic, whether it be the older citizens or the youth. We do our utmost best to accomodate all ages in our barber shop, with special rates for senior citzens every weekday from 9am to 3pm.
